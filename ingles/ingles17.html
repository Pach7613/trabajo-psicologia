I am Isaías. Yesterday, I watched a documentary about Mount Vesuvius, a volcano in Italy. The documentary explained that many years ago, Vesuvius erupted and destroyed the city of Pompeii. I got quite worried because I live in a town called Tazacorte, on the island of La Palma. There are also volcanoes here. But I have never seen one erupt... and I don't think I would like to see it.
