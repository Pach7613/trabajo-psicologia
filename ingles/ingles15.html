1 Read this text about body language.

Sometimes, people don't need to tell us how they feel. We can know just by looking at them. We also express our feelings and emotions with facial expressions and through body movements and postures. Have you ever seen a mime perform? Mimes imitate actions using only gestures and movements. It looks like they are climbing a ladder, smelling a flower, or pushing a very heavy box. But there is no ladder, flower, or box! Anyone can do mime. To imitate a dog, we get down on all fours, and to show that we are flying like an airplane, we stretch out our arms and mimic its flight. It's a lot of fun! We also send messages using gestures. For example, to signal silence, we place our index finger in front of our lips, and to ask to speak in class, we raise our hand. And we tell someone that we love them by giving them a big hug.
