An amazing camping trip

The big night had arrived. Alberto and his friends were nervous because they were going to sleep in a tent in the garden. Even Mol, Alberto’s dog, was running and barking from one side to the other without stopping.

Nela brought some blankets and spread them out on the grass. They sat on them and looked around: the darkness was almost complete.

Alberto’s mother asked:

— Do you want me to turn on the entrance light so you can see better?

— Yes — the group answered in chorus.

Sara suggested in a mysterious tone:

— We could tell horror stories.

The girls and boys felt a shiver run down their backs, but they said yes. Nobody wanted to show they were afraid.

Suddenly… shhhhhh! crrrrrrr! The garden filled with strange noises that sounded like monster howls.

— What was that? —asked Gonzalo, frightened.
