We learn languages

Reading

1 Listen to this dialogue. Then, read.

Gloria: — Hello, Greta!

Greta: — ¡Hola!, ciao, salut, hallo!

Gloria: — What did you say? I don't understand anything.

Greta: — I said hello.

Gloria: — You said hello? Well, I didn't hear that.

Greta: — That's because I said it in Spanish, Italian, French, and German.

Gloria: — But do you know how to speak all those languages?

Greta: — Not yet, but I have decided that I am going to learn.

Gloria: — And why do you want to learn them?

Greta: — Because, when I grow up, I am going to travel the whole wide world. I will visit all the places they talk about at school. And I thought that if I want to communicate with the people I meet on my travels, first I need to learn their language.

Gloria: — You're right! I would love to travel with you. But I don't know any other languages.

Greta: — If you want, we can learn together. Does that sound good to you?

Gloria: — That would be wonderful! Shall we start right now?

Greta: — ¡Por supuesto!
