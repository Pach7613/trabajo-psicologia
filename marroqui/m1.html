    Ghadi tktachaf kif dayr l-ʿalam mn khilal qraʾt mokhtalaf anwaʿ dyal nṣoṣ.

  • 05

    Ghadi tchouf ta9addom dyalek w tqayem taʿallom dyalek.

F l-idafa, ghad l-itkhdem f l-fariq dyalek.
