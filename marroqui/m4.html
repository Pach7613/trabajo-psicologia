Takhyeem wa3er bzzaf

— “Raho jayi men tma,” Alberto wwer b sbe3o j-jiha dyal d-dar.

Kolshi shaf fin kanywer l-dri, walakin ma shafo waloo. Saúl shaf shi khyalat kyt7arko o kykhey3o f l-7ayt dyal l-7jra o sweel:

— “Shno had l-khyalat?”

— “Kyban b7al jnoon!” Nela qaltha o hiya m3jba.

— “Kyban b7al l-3amaliqa bydin t-wal bzzaf,” Sara hmsat.

— “Hadshi kyrj3ni khayfa bzzaf…” Nela qaltha o hiya ktr3ed.

L-wlad o l-bnat 3anqo b3diyat-hom. Kano qrab l-ighwto melli shafo wa7ed l-khyal m3roof. Kana l-iban b7al… l-khyal dyal l-klb ky7rrek z-zekka dyalo!

— “Hada Mol!” Alberto ghwte.

— “Daba 3rft shno waqe3,” Nela d7kat o fshrat. “L-dwa dyal l-bab raho msh3ool, o had l-khyalat l-ghriba li kynshofo f l-7ayt rahom l-khyalat dyal l-shjar.”

— “O melli kyhbob r-ree7, kyban b7al l-wo7oosh kyt7arko bydihom o kyghwto!” Gonzalo t-neffes chwiya o rta7. Sara hddrat men b3d:

— “Wash n-l3bo l-masra7 dyal l-khyalat?”

— “Iyehhh!” l-akhrin ghwto o rahom far7anin. “Ghad l-ikono l-khwans zwinin.”

“Daba hada takhyeem wa3er bzzaf!” Alberto fkkor f dakhil dyalo.
