Qraya

Ana Isaías. L-bare7 tferrejt f wa3ed l-film wata2iqi 3la jbel Vesuvius, lli hiya wa3ed l-borkan f Italia. L-wata2iqi fser beli hadi bzzaf d l-snin, Vesuvius ttefrqe3 o hdem l-mdeena dyal Pompeii. Ttshwshet shewiya hit ana kantsken f wa3ed l-mdeena sghira smيتها Tazacorte, f l-jazeera dyal La Palma. Ta hna kaynin l-brakeen. Walakin 3mmri ma shoft shi wa3ed kytfrqe3... o ma kandennhsh beli ghad y3jebni nshoufo.
