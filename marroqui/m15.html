Tqrayet nass mofeed

Qraya

1 Qra had l-nass 3la l-lugha dyal l-jesd.

Ba3d l-merrat, l-nas ma ky7tajoush yqolo lina b shno kym7so. Nqdro n3rfouha ghir melli nshoufo fihom. Ta 7na knbiyyno l-masha3ir dyalna b l-malame7 d l-wjeth o b l-7arakat o l-glessa dyal l-jesd. Wash 3mmrek shofti shi mimo kyt7rrek? L-mimos kyqldo l-7arakat ghir b l-isharat o l-jesd. Kyban b7al ila kytl3o f l-slloom, kysmmo shi warda, o we dff3o shi sandouq tqeel bzzaf. Walakin makayn la slloom, la warda, la sandouq! Ay wa3ed yqder ydir l-mimo. Bash nqldo wa3ed l-kelb, knhbto foq rkabyna o iddina d l-orba3, o bash nbiyyno beli knt-tiro b7al wa3ed l-tiyara, knbsetto iddina o knqldo l-tayer dyalha. Had shi fih l-neshat bzzaf! Ta 7na kntsefthoo rsayel b l-gestes. Mthalan, bash nshwro 3la l-skat, kndiro sbe3na l-sghir (l-shahada) qddam shfayfna, o bash ntlbo l-hdra f l-qism, knhzzo iddna. O knqolo l-shi wa3ed beli knbghioh melli kndiro lih wa3ed l-t3niqa kbeera.
