Tqrayet l-diario dyal l-3otla

Ittisal

2 Jweb.

Ma3ash bdat Julia kttraine f l-sba7?

Ma3ash salat f l-3shya?

Ayna lon f l-bisiklet dyal Julia?

3 Settegh 3la l-so2al lli f l-diario dyal Julia. Men b3d, kteb l-as2ila dyal had l-ajwiba.

F l-3shya, Julia bdat f l-khmsa o noss.

Kfatni khmsa d l-swaye3 bash nt3llem nrekb 3la l-bisiklet.

T3llmat nrekb 3la l-bisiklet nhar 12 f dosht (August).
